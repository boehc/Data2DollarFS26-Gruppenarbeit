# Analysis Results

This folder contains the complete analysis results from the Swiss startup ecosystem study (2023-2026).

## 📊 Data Overview

**Analysis Period**: Q1 2023 - Q2 2026 (14 quarters)  
**Total Startups**: 1,327 companies  
**Data Sources**: YCombinator, VentureKick, StartupTicker, VClense, Kaggle

## 📁 CSV Files Description

### Core Analysis Files (Step-by-Step)

1. **STEP_A_industry_deal_counts.csv** - Deal count per industry per quarter
2. **STEP_B_industry_funding_totals.csv** - Total funding amounts by industry and quarter
3. **STEP_C_industry_deal_stages.csv** - Investment stages distribution per industry
4. **STEP_D_industry_news_volume.csv** - News/article mentions per industry
5. **STEP_E_growth_momentum_matrix.csv** - Deal volume change vs. funding momentum
6. **STEP_F_tech_innovation_matrix.csv** - Technology maturity vs. innovation scores

### Analytical Summaries

7. **SUMMARY_deal_count_trends.csv** - Deal count trends with growth rates
8. **SUMMARY_funding_trends.csv** - Funding trends with percentage changes
9. **SUMMARY_stage_distribution.csv** - Comprehensive stage distribution analysis
10. **SUMMARY_news_volume_trends.csv** - News volume analysis with trend indicators

### Easy-to-Read Reports

11. **TOP_WINNERS_LOSERS_by_deal_count.csv** - Industries with highest/lowest deal growth
12. **TOP_WINNERS_LOSERS_by_funding.csv** - Industries with highest/lowest funding changes
13. **INVESTMENT_RECOMMENDATIONS_early_stage.csv** - Top early-stage investment opportunities
14. **INVESTMENT_RECOMMENDATIONS_late_stage.csv** - Top late-stage investment opportunities
15. **NEWS_HYPE_ANALYSIS.csv** - Media attention vs. actual deal activity
16. **COMBINED_OPPORTUNITY_SCORES.csv** - Overall investment opportunity rankings

## 🔑 Key Findings

### 🚀 Top Growing Industries
- **GenAI & LLM Tools**: +168% growth momentum
- **HR & Recruiting Tech**: 85.7% in seed/early stage
- **IoT Solutions**: 78.6% in seed/early stage

### 💰 Funding Leaders
- **BioTech & MedTech**: 30.9% market share
- **FinTech & InsurTech**: 19.2% market share
- **Enterprise SaaS**: 10.8% market share

### 📉 Declining Sectors
- **ClimateTech & Sustainability**: -7.1 percentage points drop
- **Social Networks & Community**: Limited activity

### 🎯 Investment Opportunities

**Early-Stage Winners** (High growth + Early stage dominance):
1. GenAI & LLM Tools
2. HR & Recruiting Tech
3. IoT Solutions

**Late-Stage Powerhouses** (High growth + Late stage presence):
1. BioTech & MedTech
2. FinTech & InsurTech
3. Enterprise SaaS
